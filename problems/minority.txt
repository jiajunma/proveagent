*** Problem Statement ***

The Minority Game

Consider a symmetric game with three players. Each player must simultaneously choose one of two rooms: Room A or Room B.The Payoff Rule:A player wins $1$ if they are the only person in their chosen room. If more than one person chooses the same room, all players in that room receive a payoff of $0$.


Problem
1. Compute all Pure Nash Equilibria. 
2. Compute all mixed Nash Equilibria.
